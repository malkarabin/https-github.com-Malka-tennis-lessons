Locate the section described by the user.

This is usually a major screen section such as:

* hero section
* feature section
* showcase section
* landing section

Apply a subtle parallax effect while preserving the original content, layout, and design direction.

Rules:

* do not change text content
* do not redesign the section
* keep the effect subtle and professional
* preserve readability at all times

Parallax guidelines:

* create depth by making background and foreground layers move at slightly different speeds
* keep the motion restrained and elegant
* avoid dramatic or distracting movement
* maintain clear visual hierarchy

Motion discipline:

* prioritize smooth scrolling behavior
* avoid excessive animation
* ensure the section still feels stable and usable

Accessibility and usability:

* do not reduce readability of headings, text, or buttons
* preserve usability on modern responsive layouts
* keep the effect supportive, not dominant
