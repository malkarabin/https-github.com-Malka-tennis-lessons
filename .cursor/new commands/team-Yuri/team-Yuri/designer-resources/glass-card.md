Locate the card, panel, or container described by the user.

Apply a glassmorphism effect while preserving the original layout and content.

Rules:

* keep the existing colors
* do not change layout structure
* do not modify text content

Glass effect guidelines:

* introduce subtle transparency
* apply soft background blur to create depth
* add a thin border to separate the surface from the background
* add a soft shadow to enhance floating appearance

Design discipline:

* keep the effect subtle and elegant
* maintain readability of all text
* avoid excessive blur or heavy decoration
