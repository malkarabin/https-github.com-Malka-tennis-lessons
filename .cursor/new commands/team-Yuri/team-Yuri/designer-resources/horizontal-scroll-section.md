Locate the section described by the user that contains multiple visual items.

This may include:

* feature cards
* product examples
* image galleries
* showcase sections
* horizontal content groups

Convert the section into a horizontal scrolling experience while preserving the existing design direction.

Rules:

* do not change the text content
* do not redesign the individual items
* maintain readability and usability
* keep the layout stable and responsive

Horizontal scroll guidelines:

* arrange items horizontally instead of vertically
* allow users to scroll sideways to explore additional items
* maintain consistent spacing between items
* ensure the first items remain clearly visible

Interaction discipline:

* keep the experience smooth and intuitive
* avoid excessive animation
* preserve accessibility and responsive behavior

Design discipline:

* keep the section visually balanced
* ensure the horizontal layout does not overwhelm the screen
* maintain clear hierarchy between the section title and the items
