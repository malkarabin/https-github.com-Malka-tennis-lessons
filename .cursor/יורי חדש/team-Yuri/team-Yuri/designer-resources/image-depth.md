Locate the image, visual asset, or media container described by the user.

This may include:

* hero image
* product image
* illustration
* visual card image
* media block within a section

Apply an image depth effect while preserving the original layout, content, and design direction.

Rules:

* do not replace the image unless explicitly requested
* do not redesign the surrounding layout
* keep the effect subtle and professional
* preserve readability of nearby text and actions

Image depth guidelines:

* increase the sense of separation between the image and the background
* enhance visual focus on the image
* create a more layered and premium appearance
* use depth in a restrained way

Design discipline:

* avoid excessive blur, glow, or dramatic decoration
* keep the image visually stable
* preserve the balance between the image and surrounding elements
* ensure the image supports the hierarchy of the screen

Accessibility and usability:

* do not reduce clarity of the visual content
* preserve usability on responsive layouts
* keep the effect supportive, not distracting
