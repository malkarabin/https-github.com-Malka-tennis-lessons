Rules:

* do not change text content
* do not redesign the component
* do not change the overall layout structure

Floating card effect guidelines:

* increase visual separation from the background
* use depth through shadow rather than decoration
* make the card feel slightly elevated
* preserve a clean and professional appearance

Spacing and structure:

* maintain balanced internal padding
* keep content alignment clear and readable
* ensure the card remains visually stable and not overly dramatic

Design discipline:

* keep the effect subtle
* avoid exaggerated shadows
* avoid unnecessary blur or glow unless explicitly requested
* preserve readability and usability
