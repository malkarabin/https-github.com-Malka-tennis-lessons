Locate the section, component group, or UI elements described by the user.

This may include:

* cards in a section
* feature items
* text blocks
* images
* content groups within a screen

Apply a subtle scroll reveal effect while preserving the original layout, content, and design direction.

Rules:

* do not change text content
* do not redesign the UI
* keep the effect subtle and professional
* preserve readability and usability

Scroll reveal guidelines:

* reveal elements as they enter the viewport
* use gentle fade, slight movement, or soft elevation
* keep the motion restrained and elegant
* preserve the visual hierarchy of the screen

Motion discipline:

* avoid exaggerated movement
* avoid long or distracting animation
* keep transitions smooth and responsive
* ensure the UI still feels stable and premium

Accessibility and usability:

* do not reduce readability of text or actions
* preserve clarity of interactive elements
* keep the effect supportive, not dominant
