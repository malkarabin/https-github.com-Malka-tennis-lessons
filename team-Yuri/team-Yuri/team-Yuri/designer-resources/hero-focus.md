Locate the hero section or primary header section of the screen.

This is typically the first section of a page and may contain:

* the main headline
* supporting text
* primary call to action
* hero image or visual

Enhance the visual focus of the hero section while preserving the existing layout, content, and design direction.

Rules:

* do not change the text content
* do not redesign the layout
* preserve the current style and theme
* maintain readability and usability

Hero focus guidelines:

* strengthen the visual hierarchy of the main headline
* ensure the call-to-action is clearly visible
* improve the balance between text and visual elements
* increase visual emphasis on the primary message

Layout discipline:

* maintain generous spacing around the hero content
* keep the section visually calm and focused
* ensure the hero feels like the entry point of the experience

Design discipline:

* avoid excessive decoration
* avoid distracting motion
* prioritize clarity, focus, and visual impact
