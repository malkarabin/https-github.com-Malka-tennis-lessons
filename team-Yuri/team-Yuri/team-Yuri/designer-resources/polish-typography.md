Locate the UI component, section, or screen described by the user.

Improve typography while preserving the existing design style and layout.

Rules:

* do not change the content
* do not replace fonts unless explicitly requested
* maintain the original design direction

Improve the following aspects:

Text hierarchy:

* ensure clear hierarchy between headings, subheadings, and body text
* ensure headings stand out from surrounding text
* maintain logical structure of titles and descriptions

Readability:

* ensure comfortable line-height for paragraphs and text blocks
* avoid overly tight text lines
* ensure text blocks are easy to scan

Letter spacing:

* avoid excessive letter-spacing
* ensure letter spacing remains subtle and readable

Spacing around text:

* improve spacing between headings and paragraphs
* ensure text blocks have sufficient breathing room

Consistency:

* maintain consistent typography patterns across similar elements
* keep text visually balanced within containers

Important:

* preserve layout structure
* prioritize readability and visual clarity
