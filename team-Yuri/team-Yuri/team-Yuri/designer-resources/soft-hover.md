Locate the interactive UI element described by the user.

This may include:

* buttons
* cards
* links
* clickable panels
* navigation items

Apply a soft hover interaction while preserving the original design and layout.

Rules:

* do not change text content
* do not redesign the component
* keep the interaction subtle and professional

Hover behavior guidelines:

* introduce a small visual response when the cursor hovers over the element
* use gentle scale or elevation to indicate interactivity
* slightly increase shadow depth if the element already has elevation
* maintain smooth transition timing

Motion discipline:

* keep motion short and responsive
* avoid exaggerated animation
* maintain a clean and premium feel

Accessibility:

* ensure hover feedback clearly indicates interactivity
* preserve readability and contrast
